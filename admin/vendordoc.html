Online FishCart Help

Context Sensitive Help

If you have a Javascript enabled browser, just click into the field that you need help on, then roll your mouse over the Help button; a context sensitive screen will pop up automatically for that field.

If your browser does not support Javascript, then just click on the Help button and find the particular help section you need.

Vendor Contact Information

  • Enter the information for normal customer contacts.
  • A PO box address is acceptable for this information.
  • Include phone and fax numbers for information contacts.
  • Include an email address for information contacts.

Vendor Customer Service Information

  • Enter the information for Customer Service contacts in these fields.
  • Include a street address for product returns.
  • Include a Customer Service phone and fax number.
  • Include a Customer Service e-mail address.
